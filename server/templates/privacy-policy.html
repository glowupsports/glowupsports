Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

📜 Introduction

Glow Up Sports ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Glow Up Sports mobile application, based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile details when you create an account.
  • Player Information: Tennis skill levels, training history, session attendance, and progress data.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our in-app chat feature between coaches and players.
  • Payment Information: Billing details and transaction history for lesson bookings and packages.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and push notification tokens.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data, feature usage patterns, and session analytics.

 Sign in with Apple

Our app supports Sign in with Apple as an authentication method. When you choose to sign in with Apple:

  • We receive and store your Apple User ID for authentication purposes only.
  • We may receive your name and email address if you choose to share them during the sign-in process.
  • Apple may provide a private relay email address to protect your personal email. We will use whichever email is provided.
  • Your Apple ID credentials are never stored on our servers. Authentication is handled securely through Apple's identity service.
  • The Apple User ID is used solely to link your Apple account to your existing Glow Up Sports account.

We do not track, profile, or share your Apple Sign-In data with any third parties. Your Apple User ID is used exclusively for secure authentication.

📷 Camera Permission

Our app requests camera access for the following purposes:

  • Profile photo capture and updates
  • Academy and court photo uploads (for academy owners)
  • Document scanning for player registration
  • Social post media uploads

Camera access is entirely optional. You can deny this permission and the app will still function normally, but photo-related features will be limited.

🔔 Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver push notifications to your device. Notifications may include:

  • Session reminders and schedule updates
  • Feedback and performance reviews from coaches
  • Achievement unlocks, level-ups, and milestone celebrations
  • Important announcements from your academy
  • Community updates and social interactions

Push notifications are optional. You can enable or disable them at any time through your device settings. We store your device push token securely and use it solely for delivering notifications through Firebase Cloud Messaging.

🛠 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send push notifications about sessions, feedback, and achievements
  • Track player progress and generate performance analytics via the Glow Score Engine
  • Facilitate communication between coaches, players, and parents
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, and support
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

🔒 Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Secure database storage with access controls and monitoring
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only

👥 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Tennis Academies: Coaches and academy owners you are associated with can view relevant player data.
  • Parents/Guardians: Parents can view their child's progress, sessions, and performance data through the Parent Dashboard.
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our platform (see Third-Party Services section below).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.

🌎 International Data Transfers

Glow Up Sports serves users internationally, including in Dubai (United Arab Emirates) and Indonesia. As a result:

  • Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence.
  • We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
  • By using our app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries where we and our service providers operate.
  • We comply with applicable data protection regulations in all jurisdictions where we operate, including UAE data protection requirements.

📅 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your active account.
  • Training and performance data is retained to maintain your historical progress records.
  • Transaction records are retained as required by applicable financial regulations.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

To delete your account and all associated data, visit our Account Deletion page. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

⚖ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated data via our deletion page.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for optional features like camera access and push notifications at any time.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

📚 GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

Glow Up Sports acts as the data controller for the personal data collected through the app. This means we determine the purposes and means of processing your personal data and are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws.

  • We process your data based on legitimate interests, contractual necessity, or your explicit consent.
  • You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
  • We will respond to any data subject access request within 30 days.
  • We do not engage in automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects.

🇺🇸 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with additional rights:

  • Right to Know: You can request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app may be used by minors with parental or guardian consent. Parents and guardians can:

  • Create and manage their child's account through the Parent Dashboard.
  • View their child's training progress, sessions, and performance data.
  • Control their child's privacy settings and data sharing preferences.
  • Request deletion of their child's account and data at any time.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected data from a child without proper consent, please contact us immediately.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
  • Sending a notification through the app for significant changes.
  • Requiring renewed consent where necessary under applicable law.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
