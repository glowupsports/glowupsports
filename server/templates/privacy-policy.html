Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Introduction

Glow Up Sports ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Glow Up Sports mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile details when you create an account.
  • Player Information: Tennis skill levels, training history, session attendance, and progress data.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our in-app chat feature between coaches and players.
  • Payment Information: Billing details and transaction history for lesson bookings and packages.

Camera Permission

Our app requests camera access for the following purposes:

  • Profile photo capture and updates
  • Academy and court photo uploads (for academy owners)
  • Document scanning for player registration

Camera access is optional and you can deny this permission. The app will still function, but photo-related features will be limited.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send push notifications about sessions, feedback, and achievements
  • Track player progress and generate performance analytics
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, and support
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers and transmitted using encryption protocols.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Tennis Academies: Coaches and academy owners you are associated with can view relevant player data.
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our platform (hosting, email, payments).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for optional features like camera access

Children's Privacy

Our app may be used by minors with parental consent. Parents/guardians can manage their child's account and have access to view their progress and data through the Parent Dashboard.

Push Notifications

We send push notifications for session reminders, feedback from coaches, achievement unlocks, and important updates. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
