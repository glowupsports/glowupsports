1) RED LEVEL CARDS (UX-ready)
🔴 RED 3 — “Starter”

Identity (kid language): “Ik kan de bal raken en samen spelen.”
Court/ball: Red foam / 75% low compression, mini court (36’)

A) What you must be able to do (6 pillars)

Technique

FH Contact: 8/10 zachte feeds over net (coach feed)

BH Contact: 6/10 zachte feeds over net

Ready grip: “racket klaar” bij start van drill (habit)

Tactical

“Over the net” intent: kiest altijd voor veilig terugspelen

“Home spot”: na slag terug naar basispositie (met cue)

Physical

ABC: stop-start, balans na slag (geen vallen)

Catch & throw baseline: kan 5/10 ballen vangen + naar target gooien

Mental

Volgt 1-staps instructies 80% van de tijd

Blijft meedoen (geen rage/stoppen) in game blocks

Social

Wachten op beurt + high five na game

Respecteert “coach says go” (groepdiscipline)

Match

1 fun match event afgerond (mini points 7)

B) How you get there (lessons)

70% games, 30% micro-drills

Focus: contact + ready position + fun rally

C) Trial tests (max 3)

RED3_CONTACT_GATE: 10 feeds FH/BH gemixt → min 14/20 in play

RED3_BEHAVIOR_GATE: 10 min games → no quit / follows rules

RED3_FUN_MATCH: speelt 1 mini match tot 7 (score hulp ok)

D) Match format

Mini points to 7 (serve = underhand allowed)

E) Social goals

“Turn taking” + “High five”

“Good try!” 1x zeggen tegen iemand (gamified)

F) Level-up reward

Badge: “Red Starter Unlocked”

Unlock: “Sticker progress bar” + new emote

🔴 RED 2 — “Builder”

Identity: “Ik kan kort rally’en en begin te serveren.”

A) What you must be able to do

Technique

Cooperative rally: 6+ ballen met coach/peer (reset 1x max)

Serve intro: overhand throw-to-hit concept → 5/10 in service box (mid-court)

Volley intro: 6/10 zachte volleys over net vanaf service line

Tactical

Baseline positions: weet waar te staan bij serve/return

“Away from opponent”: 3/10 ballen richting open ruimte (simpel)

Physical

Split-step intro: 60% van feeds “ready bounce”

Side shuffle mini court: kan links/rechts bewegen zonder kruisen (basis)

Mental

Coachability: na 1 cue probeert het direct (min 2x per sessie)

Speelt tie-break tot 7 zonder meltdown (met hulp ok)

Social

Team behavior: helpt 1 andere speler (ballen rapen / compliment)

“No-show policy”: begrijpt “als je niet komt, geen make-up” (parent view)

Match

Min 3 match events logged (fun matches ok)

Effort flag: coach markeert “tried hard” ≥ 2 events

B) How you get there

50% games, 50% structured blocks

Focus: rally consistency + serve intro + simple points rules

C) Trial tests

RED2_RALLY_GATE: 10 min rally station → min 3 rallies of 6+

RED2_SERVE_GATE: 10 serves → min 5 in (mid-court)

RED2_MATCH_GATE: 3 match logs (auto) + effort flag

D) Match format

Mini set: first to 11 points (switch at 6)

Serve: overhand encouraged, underhand allowed

E) Social goals

“Teammate boost”: geef 2 high fives + 1 compliment

“Queue rules” (group culture)

F) Reward

Badge: “Red Builder”

Unlock: “Clan-ready” (kan in squads verschijnen)

🔴 RED 1 — “Graduate”

Identity: “Ik kan echte punten spelen, serveren en ben klaar voor Orange.”

A) What you must be able to do

Technique

Rally: 10+ met peer/coach (min 2 rallies)

Serve baseline red court: 10 serves → min 6 in (overhand)

Return: 6/10 returns in play (soft serve)

Volley + overhead exposure: volley 6/10, overhead attempt 3/10 (control > power)

Tactical

Simple point plan: “rally cross → open space” (coach sees intent)

Recovery: komt terug naar center zonder reminder 70%

Score: kan mini scoring zelfstandig (met kleine check)

Physical

Covers full red court: wide ball + recover 6/10

Endurance: blijft engaged 45–60 min (age-appropriate)

Footwork habit: split-step verschijnt automatisch 70%

Mental

Reset routine: na fout → “shake it off” binnen 5 sec (no drama)

Pressure: speelt 1 tie-break met focus tot einde

Social

Group integration: kan in nieuwe groep instromen zonder issues

Sportsmanship: eerlijk calls + handshake/high five na match

Match

Min 6 match events logged

Min 2 wins OR “dominance flag” (coach + system confirms)

Trial-ready: stable performance 2 weken

B) How you get there

40% drills, 40% point play, 20% games

Focus: serve/return, patterns, match readiness

C) Trial tests

RED1_SERVE_RETURN_GATE: 10 serves + 10 returns → min 12/20 in play

RED1_RALLY_GATE: 2 rallies van 10+

RED_GRAD_MATCH: 1 scored match (best of 3 short sets OR race to 21)

D) Match format

Best of 3 short sets to 4 games (tiebreak at 3-3) of race to 21

E) Social goals

“Captain moment”: helpt set-up / encourages team

“Matchmaking unlocked”: kan via app spelers vinden (supervised)

F) Reward

Badge: “Red Graduate 🏅”

Unlock: Orange UI language + Orange templates + new cosmetics

3) RED LESSON TEMPLATES (progress-generating, coach-light)
Template regels (zodat coach geen tijd verliest)

Coach kiest: Session goal + (optioneel) intensity

App weet: private/group/semi + roster levels + vorige focus

Template levert: blocks + timers + skill tags + expected success range

Post-session: app toont automatisch 3 skills om te raten (0/1/2)

“Success range” principe (cheat-proof)

Coach hoeft niet exact te tellen, maar kiest:

Below range / In range / Above range

App vertaalt dit naar score delta.
