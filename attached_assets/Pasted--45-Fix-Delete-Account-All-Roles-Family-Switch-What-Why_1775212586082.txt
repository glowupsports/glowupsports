#45 - Fix Delete Account — All Roles + Family Switch
What & Why
Two things need fixing:

DELETE /api/player/me/account rejects any account that isn't a pure player role — coaches, owners, admins and platform owners all get a 403 when trying to delete their own account.
There is no way to fully act as a family member's account. The user wants to tap a family member in the Family section and be completely switched into that player's session — so all their settings, including "Delete My Account", work naturally. A visible "Switch Back" button returns the user to their original account.
Done looks like
In the Family section, tapping a family member's card fully switches the active session to that player (new auth token / session for that player is loaded)
A persistent "Switch Back" bar or banner is shown while acting as a family member so the user knows they can return
Tapping "Switch Back" restores the original account session
While switched in as a family member (a pure player account), the existing "Delete My Account" in their Settings works without any changes
A coach, owner, admin, or platform owner can tap "Delete Account" in their own Settings screen, confirm, and their account is deleted — they are returned to the login screen (currently blocked by the role check on the backend)
Out of scope
Adding a separate "delete child" endpoint (not needed — switching in as them makes their own delete work)
Changing anything about the player self-delete flow
Hard deletes — always soft-delete + anonymize
Tasks
Backend: family account switch endpoint — Add POST /api/family/switch/:playerId that verifies the target player belongs to the caller's family, then issues a scoped session token for that player account. Also add POST /api/family/switch-back that returns the original account's session. Store the "original session" reference server-side or in a signed token so it can't be spoofed.

Backend: fix own-account deletion for non-player roles — Remove the role === "player" guard from DELETE /api/player/me/account. Look up the player record by userId regardless of role and run the same soft-delete + PII wipe + email notification flow.

UI: Family screen — switch into account — In the Family member list, make tapping a member trigger the switch endpoint. On success, reload the app session as that player. Store a flag (e.g. in SecureStore) that the user is in a "switched" state with the original token saved.

UI: Switch Back banner — When the app detects the user is in a switched family session, show a persistent bar at the top (or a clear button in the header/settings) labelled with the original account name. Tapping it calls switch-back, restores the token, and reloads the session.

UI: Delete Account for coach/owner/admin settings — Add the "Delete Account" pressable below the Log Out button in the coach, owner/admin, and platform settings screens with the same 2-step confirmation pattern as the player settings screen.
