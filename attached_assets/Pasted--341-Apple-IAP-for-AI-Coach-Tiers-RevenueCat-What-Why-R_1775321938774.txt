#341 - Apple IAP for AI Coach Tiers (RevenueCat)
What & Why
Replace the existing Stripe-based AI Pro upgrade flow with Apple In-App Purchases via RevenueCat. Players currently see a modal that redirects to a Stripe web checkout — Apple requires subscriptions for digital features to go through their native IAP system. This unlocks the full native payment experience and eliminates friction of leaving the app.

The AI coach has a 5-call free tier enforced on the backend. Pro subscribers get unlimited AI calls per month.

App details:

Bundle ID: com.glowupsports.app
App Store Connect subscription group: "Ai Coach" (ID: 22014049)
User must create in App Store Connect (inside the "Ai Coach" group):
com.glowupsports.app.aipro.monthly — $9.99/month
com.glowupsports.app.aipro.yearly — $79.99/year
RevenueCat entitlement identifier: ai_pro
Done looks like
RevenueCat integration connected, seed script run successfully, env vars set
client/lib/revenuecat.tsx with SubscriptionProvider and useSubscription hook
App.tsx initializes RevenueCat and wraps app with SubscriptionProvider
AiProUpgradeModal shows monthly + yearly packages with live prices from RevenueCat, triggers Apple's native payment sheet, includes restore purchases button
Backend aiProSubscription.ts checks RevenueCat entitlements instead of Stripe
Purchases.logIn(userId) called on user login to tie purchases to account
