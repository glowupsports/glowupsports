⚠️ Wat ontbreekt (belangrijk voor Apple)

Apple let in 2026 sterk op:

1️⃣ Data retention periode

Je moet vermelden hoe lang je data bewaart.

Bijvoorbeeld:

We retain personal data as long as your account is active or as necessary to comply with legal obligations.

2️⃣ Internationale data transfers

Je moet zeggen of data buiten de EU wordt opgeslagen.

Bijvoorbeeld:

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence.

3️⃣ Third-party login (Apple Login!)

Je gebruikt Apple login.
Dat moet expliciet genoemd worden.

Bijvoorbeeld:

If you sign in using Apple, we receive limited profile information as permitted by Apple.

4️⃣ Payment processor vermelding

Je noemt “Payment Information”, maar je moet zeggen dat betalingen via third-party providers lopen.

Bijvoorbeeld:

Payments are processed securely by third-party payment providers and we do not store full credit card details.

5️⃣ Analytics / Crash reporting

Gebruik je Expo / Apple analytics / monitoring tools?
Dan moet je vermelden:

We may use analytics tools to understand app performance and usage.

🔒 Extra sectie toevoegen onder “Data Storage and Security”

Data Retention
We retain personal information for as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account at any time through the app or by contacting us.

International Data Transfers
Your information may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

🔐 Extra toevoegen onder “Information We Collect”

Third-Party Login
If you choose to sign in using Apple or other third-party providers, we receive limited profile information in accordance with the provider’s privacy settings.

Analytics and Performance Data
We may collect anonymous usage and performance data to improve the functionality, stability, and user experience of the app.

💳 Extra toevoegen onder Payment Information

Payments are processed securely by third-party payment providers. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
