1️⃣ SUBSCRIPTIONS MODULE (MVP — NO STRIPE)
Purpose

Subscriptions represent what a player SHOULD pay,
not what has already been paid.

Database: subscriptions
subscriptions
- id
- academy_id
- player_id
- plan_name
- price
- currency
- billing_period (weekly | monthly)
- sessions_per_period (optional)
- status (active | paused | cancelled)
- start_date
- end_date (nullable)
- created_at


⚠️ Important:

Subscriptions ≠ payments

Subscriptions are contracts / agreements

Pause / cancel subscription

Owner UI (read-only)

Owners can:

View all active subscriptions

See totals by plan

Understand expected revenue
