✅ Regels

Vertical = tijd

⏱️ TIME SCALE (heilig)

30 minuten of 60 minuten

vaste rowHeight (bijv. 56px)

NO auto-height

rowHeight = slotDuration === 30 ? 56 : 80

🎾 EVENTS (LESSEN)
Event block

absolute positioned

height = duration * rowHeight

top = (startTime - startOfDay) * rowHeight

🟠 Group

⚫ Blocked / Holiday
