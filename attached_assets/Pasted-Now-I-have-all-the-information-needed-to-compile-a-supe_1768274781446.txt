1. OVERVIEW OF THE WEBAPP
Purpose
The Player App is a comprehensive mobile experience for tennis players, built as part of the Glow Up Sports multi-academy SaaS platform. It combines gamified training progression, social networking, session booking, marketplace commerce, and match tracking into one unified experience. The app follows a "Solo Leveling" gaming aesthetic with XP-based progression, feature unlocking, and visual celebration systems.

Target Users
User Type	Description
Junior Players	Under-18 players with parent controls enabled
Adult Players	18+ players with full autonomy
Parent/Guardian	Dedicated portal for managing junior player's account
