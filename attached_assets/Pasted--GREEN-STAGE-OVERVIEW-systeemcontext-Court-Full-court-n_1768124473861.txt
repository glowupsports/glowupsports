1) 🟢 GREEN LEVEL CARDS (UX-ready)
🟢 GREEN 3 — “Full-Court Adapter”

Identity (player language):

“Ik kan full court spelen zonder chaos. Ik blijf in een rally en ik serveer echt.”

A) What you must be able to do

TECHNIQUE

Full-court rally: 8+ slagen (min 2x)

Depth control: 6/10 ballen voorbij service line (rally drill)

Serve: 6/10 in (full baseline)

Second serve concept: min 5 poging met duidelijke safety serve (spin/placement)

Return: 6/10 in play tegen medium serve

Volley intro: 6/10 gecontroleerd (service line → net)

TACTICAL

Recovery: terug naar “neutral zone” zichtbaar na iedere slag

Speelt cross-court als default (high percentage)

Herkent “short ball” (coach feed) en probeert approach

PHYSICAL

Split-step ≥ 70%

Kan wide ball halen + recover (min 4 reps per kant)

60 min sessie zonder collapse (energie + focus)

MENTAL

Reset routine zichtbaar na fout (1–2 sec)

Kan 3 games spelen zonder emotionele drop

Reageert op 1 cue en past het toe binnen dezelfde drill

SOCIAL

Kan zelf score roepen en eerlijk zijn

Respecteert partner/tegenstander bij fouten

MATCH

Min 3 full-court matches (short sets ok)

Min 1 match met “self-officiating” (zonder coach hulp)

B) How you get there (lessons)

40% rally depth drills

30% serve/return

30% points (short games)

C) Trial tests

G3_RALLY_GATE → 2 rallies van 8+

G3_DEPTH_GATE → 6/10 voorbij service line

G3_SERVE_GATE → 6/10 first serve + 5 safe seconds

G3_MATCH_LOG → 3 matches

D) Match format

Set to 4 games (TB at 3–3)

Deuce = deciding point

E) Social goals

“Self ref” badge: eerlijk calls maken

Partner respect: “my bad” culture

F) Reward

Badge: Green Adapter

Unlock: “Depth meter” + “Serve % tracker”

🟢 GREEN 2 — “Point Builder”

Identity:

“Ik kan punten bouwen met diepte, richting en een plan.”

A) What you must be able to do

TECHNIQUE

Full-court rally: 12+ slagen (min 2x)

Depth: 7/10 voorbij service line (structured drill)

Serve: 7/10 in (first serve)

Second serve: 6/10 in (veilig, geen double-fault spam)

Return: 7/10 in play + min 3/10 returns diep (past service line)

Net skills: volleys links/rechts 7/10

Overhead: 5/10 controlled finish

TACTICAL

Pattern tennis:

cross → cross → change direction (min 2x per set)

Speelt naar zwakte (BH-targeting) zichtbaar in match

Begrijpt when-to-attack vs when-to-rally

PHYSICAL

Kan baseline-to-net transition (approach + split + volley) herhalen

Endurance: 90 min training / 1 match zonder grote drop

Footwork: crossover step + recovery consistent

MENTAL

Point routines: serve routine + return routine consistent

Speelt tie-break zonder meltdown

Kan “focus target” houden (bv. 70% first serves) in match

SOCIAL

Lost score conflict op zonder drama

Kan doubles basics (communicatie “mine/yours”)

MATCH

Min 6 matches full court

Min 2 wins (of “competitive matches” badge als net niet wint)

Min 1 doubles match

B) How you get there

30% technique

40% pattern points

30% match simulation

C) Trial tests

G2_RALLY_GATE → 2 rallies van 12+

G2_SERVE_RETURN_GATE → 14/20 in play + 3 deep returns

G2_SECOND_SERVE_GATE → 6/10 in

G2_MATCH_GATE → 6 matches + 2 wins OR competitiveness flag

D) Match format

Best of 3 short sets

TB to 7

E) Social goals

Doubles etiquette + communication

Positive leader in group

F) Reward

Badge: Green Builder

Unlock: “Pattern tracker” (serve+1 / rally+change)

🟢 GREEN 1 — “Pre-Yellow Graduate”

Identity:

“Ik speel echte tennis op full court met controle, 2e serve, en mentale stabiliteit.”

A) What you must be able to do

TECHNIQUE

Full-court rally: 18+ slagen (min 1x)

Depth consistency: 8/10 voorbij service line (controlled rally drill)

Serve: 8/10 in (first serve) + target zones (wide/body/T)

Second serve: 7/10 in under pressure drill

Return: 8/10 in play + 5/10 returns met richting (BH/FH target)

Volleys: 8/10 + transition volley

Overhead: 7/10 finish

Variation intro: slice OR drop shot competence (min 5/10 success in drill)

TACTICAL

Gameplan zichtbaar + aanpassen als het niet werkt

Serve +1 patroon (min 3 keer per match)

Defense → neutral → attack (3-phase point) herkenbaar

Begrijpt risico: high % tennis op belangrijke punten

PHYSICAL

Kan 2 matches per dag aan (energie + movement blijft)

Speed: eerste stap explosief, recovery blijft netjes

Injury habits: warm-up routine + cooldown (app checklist)

MENTAL

Pressure proof:

sluit set/match af zonder imploding

kan down zijn en toch vechten (comeback behavior)

Self-coaching: benoemt eigen oplossing (“meer hoogte”, “dieper”)

Consistent attitude (no tantrums)

SOCIAL

Rolmodel gedrag standaard

Helpt jongere spelers / team vibe

MATCH

Min 10 matches full court

Min 4 wins (of “dominant at green” flag door evidence)

Min 1 event: ladder / club tournament / league

Coach confirms: “Outgrown green level”

B) How you get there

20% technique tune

50% match play

30% review + goals

C) Trial tests

G1_SERVE_TEST → 15 serves: 10 in (incl 5 seconds)

G1_RETURN_TEST → 20 returns: 16 in play + 8 targeted

G1_RALLY_TEST → 1 rally 18+

G1_MATCH_CERT → 1 full match logged + maturity checklist

D) Match format

Best of 3 sets (short)

Official scoring

Self-officiating mandatory

E) Social goals

“Respect under pressure”

“Team leader”

F) Reward

Badge: Green Graduate 🟡

Unlock: YELLOW STAGE

4) Hoe dit coach-proof blijft (wereldwijd)

De regels zijn locked. Coaches voeren alleen “bewijs” in via:

snelle ratings (3 per sessie)

trials (gates)

match logs

Anti-cheat (de kern)

Level up alleen als:

trial tests gehaald zijn

match volume klopt

moving average over meerdere sessies stabiel is

1 coach kan niet “1x klikken = level up”

Als coach altijd iedereen “perfect” geeft → wordt zichtbaar in integrity metrics (later bij YELLOW spec leg ik die engine uit)
